Mountain Getaway Ideas
1. Misty Peaks
Location: Highland Valley , Thailand

Price Range: $700 - $1,000 for a 5-day trip

Hotel Listings:
Mountain Lodge: $180/night - Offers cozy cabins, a fireplace, and guided hikes.

Alpine Retreat: $150/night - Features mountain-view rooms, a spa, and a restaurant.

Summit Inn: $160/night - Includes complimentary breakfast, a hot tub, and a game room.

Travel Advice:
Hotspots: Visit the Scenic Lookout for panoramic views, hike the Emerald Trail, and explore the nearby waterfalls.

Tips: Bring warm clothing, hiking boots, and a camera. Book guided tours for a better experience.

2. Snowy Ridge
Location: Indonesia

Price Range: $900 - $1,200 for a 6-day trip

Hotel Listings:
Frosty Peaks Resort: $220/night - Features luxury suites, a ski-in/ski-out option, and a gourmet restaurant.

Glacier Lodge: $180/night - Offers comfortable rooms, a sauna, and ski rentals.

Icecap Inn: $160/night - Includes breakfast, a lounge with a fireplace, and snowshoeing tours.

Travel Advice:
Hotspots: Ski the Alpine Slopes, visit the Ice Caves, and take a snowmobile tour.

Tips: Pack thermal wear, ski gear, and sunscreen. Plan your visit during the peak winter season for the best experience.

3. Evergreen Hills
Location: Meghalaya ,India.

Price Range: $800 - $1,100 for a 5-day trip

Hotel Listings:
Pine Crest Lodge: $190/night - Offers rustic cabins, a bonfire area, and guided nature walks.

Forest Retreat: $160/night - Features forest-view rooms, a hot tub, and a restaurant.

Mountain View Inn: $170/night - Includes breakfast, a fitness center, and a sauna.

Travel Advice:
Hotspots: Explore the Pine Forest Trails, visit the Hidden Lake, and enjoy stargazing at the Summit Point.

Tips: Bring hiking gear, binoculars, and a flashlight. Check weather conditions before planning hikes.

4. Himalayan Rocky Heights
Location: Nepal.

Price Range: $950 - $1,300 for a 6-day trip

Hotel Listings:
Rocky Summit Lodge: $240/night - Features luxury cabins, a spa, and a fine dining restaurant.

Eagle's Nest Inn: $180/night - Offers spacious rooms, a hot tub, and guided climbing tours.

Stone Ridge Hotel: $170/night - Includes breakfast, a bar, and mountain bike rentals.

Travel Advice:
Hotspots: Hike the Eagle's Trail, visit the Crystal Cave, and enjoy a scenic helicopter tour.

Tips: Pack sturdy hiking boots, layered clothing, and a first aid kit. Book tours and activities in advance.

5. Maple Valley
Location: Maple Mountains , China.

Price Range: $850 - $1,200 for a 5-day trip

Hotel Listings:
Maple Lodge: $200/night - Offers cozy rooms, a fireplace, and complimentary breakfast.

Valley Retreat: $170/night - Features valley-view cabins, a sauna, and a restaurant.

Autumn Inn: $180/night - Includes breakfast, a hot tub, and guided nature walks.

Travel Advice:
Hotspots: Visit the Maple Grove, hike the Autumn Trail, and explore the Maple Falls.

Tips: Bring warm clothing, hiking gear, and a camera. Plan your visit during the autumn season for the best foliage views.
